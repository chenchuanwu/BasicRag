Remote Work Policy - Effective March 2024

1. ELIGIBILITY
   All full-time employees with 6+ months tenure are eligible for remote work up to 3 days per week. Department heads must approve schedules by the 25th of the preceding month.

2. EQUIPMENT & SECURITY
   - Company provides: laptop, monitor, keyboard, and ergonomic chair
   - Employees must use VPN for all work-related activities
   - Home network must have WPA3 encryption minimum
   - Confidential documents must be stored on encrypted drives only

3. AVAILABILITY REQUIREMENTS
   - Core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM in your local time zone
   - Must respond to messages within 30 minutes during core hours
   - Video on for all meetings with 3+ participants

4. PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS
   Remote employees are held to the same KPIs as in-office staff. Monthly check-ins with managers are mandatory. Failure to meet deliverables may result in revocation of remote privileges.

For questions, contact HR at hr@company.com.
